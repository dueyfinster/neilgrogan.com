Since last year I've had a [Framework 13](/framework13) with
[NixOS](https;//wwww.nixos.org) installed initially, using Sway and Wayland.
Over the summer, there's been a lot of publicity about Omarchy, a new spin of
Arch Linux by David Heinemier Hansson, the creator of Ruby on Rails framework.
With the flexibility to reinstall NixOS, I was unafraid to blow it all away and
try Omarchy.

## The good

Installing Omarchy was fairly straightforward, with more questions than a
typical comparable Ubuntu install.
