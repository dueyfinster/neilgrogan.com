Yesterday, I blogged about  my "GoBag v2" and before that about the original GoBag - "[Tech Go Bag](/gobag	)" in 2015. This time it's to write about my day pack, essentials for being out and about.

<!--more-->

The core of the system is a [Bellroy Venture Sling 9l](https://bellroy.com/products/venture-sling?color=black&material=baida_nylon&size=9l#slide-0) which has been fantastic and roomy for day trips. It's much roomier than you would imagine and inside I regularly have:

* [Nitecore NW5000 Magsafe Powerbank](https://www.knivesandtools.ie/en/pt/-nitecore-nw5000-magnetic-wireless-charging-powerbank-forged-carbon-fiber-5000-mah.htm) & [NiteCore 10,000 mah Power bank](https://www.knivesandtools.ie/en/pt/-nitecore-summit-10000-powerbank-10000-mah.htm)
* 2 Magnetic Cables ([USB-C](https://www.amazon.ie/MagStack-Charging-Organized-Clutter-free-Magnetic/dp/B0D45VR8ZT) and [Multi-head](https://www.amazon.ie/Statik-360-Magnetic-Charging-Cable/dp/B0CTBLG192)) - plus some
* [Kenu Stance](https://www.kenu.com/products/stance-10-in-1-smartphone-super-gadget-magsafe-universal) Magsafe Phone Stand
* 1 [Rite in the Rain Wallet Notebook](https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/B08KWPM65V)
* Pen (usually [fisher space pen](https://www.amazon.ie/Fisher-Space-Pen-Bullet-Chrome/dp/B000095K9D))
* [Clinell Hand Wipes](https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/B006H4R4D4)
* Pocket Tissues (like Kleenex) - usually Aldi or Lidl own brand
* 2-3 pieces of [compressed towels](https://www.amazon.ie/dp/B0D25V6Y5S)
* [Bidet water bottle attachment](https://www.amazon.ie/dp/B07L448T4K)
* [Nanobag](https://nanobag.com/products/nanobag-eu) - in case I need extra storage (shopping etc.)



Items I add or remove, depending:

* [Vapur Foldable water bottle](https://www.amazon.ie/Vapur-Eclipse-Flexible-Water-Bottle/dp/B00BI9AM3C) - I've had this item for years and it's a must buy.
* [Foldable Spork](https://www.amazon.ie/dp/B0B97BM8T3)
* [Ultra Light Umbrella](https://www.amazon.ie/dp/B082HPPGN4)
* [Magsafe Airplane Phone Holder](https://www.amazon.ie/dp/B0D7BWRHCY)



Similar then to my wash bag from the previous post I also have a reseable [shiny backed mylar zip lock bag](https://www.amazon.ie/dp/B07SGPY447) "first aid" kit inside the sling with:

* 1 or 2 plasters (band-aids)
* 1 small alcohol wipe
* common medications in a individual [small 2*3cm sealed zip lock bag](https://www.aliexpress.com/item/1005003816703495.html?spm=a2g0o.order_list.order_list_main.147.4e421802IGxdv7) (paracetamol, ibuprofen, immodium, anti-histamine, etc.)

For my current "pocket" carry, I have:

* [Alpaka Zip Pouch](https://alpakagear.com/products/zip-pouch) (in Orange) and inside is:
  * Cards
  * Cash
  * [Victorinox Compact 91mm Red]() (removed for international travel)
  * [RovyVon A8](https://www.amazon.ie/RovyVon-Rechargeable-Flashlight-Magnetic-Sidelights/dp/B0BNKYR3YJ) torch

* AirPods v3
* iPhone 15 Pro
