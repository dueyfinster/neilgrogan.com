Day Bag

July 14, 2026 · 2 min · Neil Grogan

Last post, I spoke about my “GoBag v2” and even before that about the original GoBag - “Tech Go Bag” back in 2015. This time it’s to write about my day pack, essentials for being out and about.

The core of the system is a Bellroy Venture Sling 9l which has been fantastic and roomy for day trips. It’s much roomier than you would imagine and inside I regularly have:

  • Nitecore NW5000 Magsafe Powerbank & NiteCore 10,000 mah Power bank
  • 2 Magnetic Cables (USB-C and Multi-head) - plus some
  • Kenu Stance Magsafe Phone Stand
  • 1 Rite in the Rain Wallet Notebook
  • Pen (usually fisher space pen)
  • Clinell Hand Wipes
  • Pocket Tissues (like Kleenex) - usually Aldi or Lidl own brand
  • 2-3 pieces of compressed towels
  • Bidet water bottle attachment
  • Nanobag - in case I need extra storage (shopping etc.)

Items I add or remove, depending:

  • Vapur Foldable water bottle - I’ve had this item for years and it’s a must buy.
  • Foldable Spork
  • Ultra Light Umbrella
  • Magsafe Airplane Phone Holder

Similar then to my wash bag from the previous post I also have a reseable shiny backed mylar zip lock bag “first aid” kit inside the sling with:

  • 1 or 2 plasters (band-aids)
  • 1 small alcohol wipe
  • common medications in a individual small 2*3cm sealed zip lock bag (paracetamol, ibuprofen, immodium, anti-histamine, etc.)

For my current “pocket” carry, I have:

  • Alpaka Zip Pouch (in Orange) and inside is:

    • Cards
    • Cash
    • Victorinox Compact 91mm Red (removed for international travel)
    • RovyVon A8 torch

  • AirPods v3

  • iPhone 15 Pro
